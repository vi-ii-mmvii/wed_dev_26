Must-visit destinations in Argentina:

  1. Perito Moreno Glacier

    2. Located in Los Glaciares National Park, this massive ice formation is one of the few glaciers in the world that is still advancing. Its towering blue walls create a thunderous spectacle as ice chunks crash into Lake Argentino.

  2. Iguazu Falls

    3. A magnificent system of 275 waterfalls on the border with Brazil. The "Devil's Throat" is its most powerful cataract, surrounded by lush subtropical rainforests teeming with exotic wildlife.
