Beef Empanadas

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredient Quantity
Ground Beef 500g
Empanada Dough Disks 12 pieces
Onions (minced) 2 large
Cumin & Paprika To taste

Steps:

1. Sauté onions and beef with spices until browned.

2. Place a spoonful of filling on each dough disk.

3. Fold and seal the edges (repulgue), then bake at 200°C for 20 mins.

Authentic Chimichurri

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 jar
Ingredient Quantity
Fresh Parsley 1 cup
Garlic Cloves 3-4 minced
Red Wine Vinegar 2 tbsp
Olive Oil 1/2 cup

Steps:

1. Finely chop the parsley and garlic.

2. Whisk with oil, vinegar, and a pinch of chili flakes.

3. Let sit for at least 2 hours before serving over grilled meat.
